Research

I am working on intersection of learning and systems. I build many impactful scalable learning systems. The real excitement of this area comes from what can enable when we bring advanced learning techniques and system together. On that end, I am also pushing the direction on deep learning, knowledge transfer and lifelong learning. I am supported by Google PhD Fellowship
