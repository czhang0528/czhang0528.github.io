About Me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University, working with Wei-Lun Harry Chao and Dong Xuan. I have also worked with Chunyi Peng (now at Purdue). Before coming to OSU, I received my M.S. and B.S. degrees from BUPT and Tianjin University, respectively.

My primary research interests are in machine learning and its applications to computer vision.
I am also interested in building multi-sensor system over new wireless (e.g., vision, light, and sound), mostly from a data-driven prospective.
