About

    I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University, co-advised by Prof. Dong Xuan and Prof. Chunyi Peng (now at Purdue). Before that, I was Master student of Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, advised by Prof. Huadong Ma. I received my bachelor's degree from Tianjin University where I was working with Prof. Wei Feng. I did internship at DeepCode Robotics, Alibaba iDST and Huawei 2012 Labs. My hometown is Huanggang, China.

Research

    My primary research interests are mobile system, robotics, and computer vision. In particular, I focus on developing novel algorithms and efficient systems for real-world applications such as surveillance, sports analytics and robotics.
