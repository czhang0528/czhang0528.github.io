About

    Since Spring 2017, I have been a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University. I work with Prof. Dong Xuan and Prof. Chunyi Peng (now at Purdue). Before that, I received M.S and B.S degrees from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications under Prof. Huadong Ma, and Tianjin University under Prof. Wei Feng, respectively. My hometown is Huanggang, China.

Research

    My primary research interests are mobile system, robotics, and computer vision, with a recent focus on developing novel algorithms and efficient systems for real-world applications such as surveillance, sports analytics and robotics.
