About Me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University, advised by Wei-Lun Harry Chao and Dong Xuan. I have also worked with Chunyi Peng (now at Purdue). Before coming to OSU, I received my M.S. and B.S. degrees from BUPT and Tianjin University, respectively. For more information, see a copy of my CV (Last update: 03/2020).

I work on machine learning and its applications to computer vision, vision and language, and mobile sensing. Particularly, I am interested in developing data-efficient algorithms to better understand objects, scenes, and their relationships.
