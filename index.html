About

    Since Spring 2017, I have been a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University. I worked with Prof. Dong Xuan and Prof. Chunyi Peng (now at Purdue). Before that, I was Master student of Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, advised by Prof. Huadong Ma. I received my bachelor's degree from Tianjin University where I was working with Prof. Wei Feng. I did internship at DeepCode Robotics, Alibaba iDST and Huawei 2012 Labs. My hometown is Huanggang, China.

Research

  • My primary research interests are mobile system, robotics, and computer vision, with a recent focus on developing novel algorithms and efficient systems for real-world applications such as surveillance, sports analytics and robotics.
  • For system implementation, my research is based on customized hardware platforms, i.e., wearable devices, camera sensors, and mobile robot platform. For theoretical modeling, my research is based on advanced signal processing, computer vision, machine learning, optimization and probability methods.
